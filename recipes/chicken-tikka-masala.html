Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken Tikka Masala is a beloved Indian-inspired dish featuring tender chunks of marinated chicken in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce. The aromatic spices and velvety texture make this dish absolutely irresistible.

This restaurant-quality recipe can be made right in your own kitchen. Serve it over fluffy basmati rice with warm naan bread for a complete meal that will transport your taste buds straight to India.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds boneless chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine yogurt, lemon juice, 1 teaspoon garam masala, cumin, and turmeric.
  2. Add chicken chunks and marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight for best results).
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade and cook until browned on all sides. Set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, saut√© onion until soft and golden.
  6. Add garlic and ginger, cooking for 1 minute until fragrant.
  7. Stir in remaining garam masala and tomato sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Add heavy cream and stir until well combined.
  9. Return chicken to the skillet and simmer for 15 minutes until fully cooked.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve over rice with naan bread.
