Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian lasagna is a hearty and comforting dish featuring layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's perfect for feeding a crowd or enjoying as leftovers throughout the week.

The combination of savory ground beef, aromatic herbs, and three types of cheese creates an irresistible flavor profile that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown ground beef with diced onion and garlic over medium heat.
  4. Drain excess fat and stir in marinara sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, half the mozzarella, Parmesan, and basil.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Layer 4 noodles, half the ricotta mixture, and one-third of remaining meat sauce.
  8. Repeat layers once more, then top with remaining noodles and meat sauce.
  9. Sprinkle remaining mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  12. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
